How it works

1

Create your form

Design your contact form with any HTML and CSS. Keep your existing styling and structure exactly as you want it.

2

Add our endpoint

Simply point your form's action to our secure endpoint. No API keys, no complex setup, no backend code required.

That's it! Form submissions are instantly delivered to your inbox. Focus on design, we handle the delivery.
